eventBass

eventBass, an EventBrite inspired project, was created to promote EDM events around Denver. The clone features full user authentication through sign up/login, with a logged in user being able to create events, as well as delete and edit those events. Logged in users can also leave comments on other user's events, and come back later to update those comments if need be!

bestBnB

bestBnB is a full stack clone of AirBnB, including full user authentication capabilities. Any user can browse different ski-in/ski-out homes throughout the Rocky Mountains, and once logged-in, a user can list their home and make changes to that listing. Logged in users can also leave reviews on homes they have stayed at. The app's database tracks a user's listings and reviews, and they can be managed from the drop down menu at any point!

Gulped

Gulped is a full stack clone of Yelp, where users can log in/sign up to become local food critics! Business owners can add their business, add menu items, and leave reviews on other business' pages. In the mood for something specific? Tell the search bar at the top what you have in mind and well show the best spots around town for it!
